# THE INHERITANCE — CONTINUE BUILDING FROM THE 360° ENVIRONMENT

## Context

The entry sequence and 360° environment are already complete.

**DO NOT redesign or modify them.**

The user has already entered a monumental open-air hall inspired by South Indian and Southeast Asian stone architecture.

The environment already contains:

* Massive carved pillars
* Open circular ceiling
* Moonlit night
* Milky Way visible above
* Deep stone textures
* Deepa niches within pillars

Continue building the experience from this point onwards.

---

# Overall Design Philosophy

This is NOT an app.

This is NOT a dashboard.

This is NOT a productivity tool.

Every interaction should feel like a ritual.

There should be more silence than motion.

More atmosphere than interface.

Every UI element should feel like it belongs inside ancient architecture.

No floating glass cards.

No modern buttons.

No neon.

No excessive gradients.

The architecture itself is the interface.

---

# Camera

The camera remains first-person.

The user stands in the center of the hall.

Very subtle idle movement.

Slow breathing motion.

Small floating dust particles.

Gentle deepa flickering.

The environment should always remain visible.

Never replace the scene with full-screen forms.

---

# Initial UI

After entering, only three icons should exist.

They should be minimal stone/etched line icons with warm amber hover states.

No labels until hovered.

Icons should remain unobtrusive.

---

## Icon 1 — Palm Leaf Manuscript

Position:
Bottom center.

Appearance:
Ancient palm leaf tied with thread.

Hover text:

"Entrust an Inheritance"

Interaction:

When clicked:

The environment gently darkens.

A palm leaf manuscript slowly materializes in front of the user.

The manuscript unfolds with a subtle animation.

The user can type directly onto the palm leaf.

Fields:

* Message
* Optional Name
* Anonymous Toggle
* Day
* Month
* Year
* Hour
* Minute (24-hour)

The date selector should feel engraved into stone rather than a modern date picker.

---

## Send

After writing:

A single warm amber button appears.

Text:

"Entrust"

NOT "Send"

When clicked:

The manuscript rolls itself shut.

A thread wraps around it.

The chosen date disappears permanently.

The manuscript slowly rises.

It pauses beneath the circular opening.

Then suddenly shoots into the night sky.

No loading screen.

No confirmation modal.

Silence.

One second later—

A single deepa somewhere on a pillar lights.

The camera subtly turns toward the newly lit flame.

---

## Icon 2 — Night Archive

Position:
Bottom left.

Appearance:
Small constellation icon.

Hover text:

"Night Archive"

When clicked:

The camera slowly rises above the hall.

The architecture disappears beneath.

The Milky Way fills the screen.

Every star represents one entrusted inheritance.

No stars:

Display:

"The universe is waiting for its first inheritance."

Stars:

Softly pulse.

Color variations represent emotional themes:

Warm Gold — Hope

Soft Amber — Love

Moon White — Wonder

Deep Indigo — Grief

Silver Blue — Forgiveness

Pale Orange — Courage

Do NOT explicitly label emotions.

Hovering over a star shows only:

Anonymous

Named

Date Entrusted

No message preview.

---

## Icon 3 — Returning Manuscripts

Position:
Top right.

Only visible when an inheritance reaches its selected date and time.

Never visible otherwise.

Appearance:

Small folded manuscript icon with a subtle warm glow.

Clicking:

The manuscript slowly materializes before the user.

It unfolds naturally.

The inheritance becomes readable.

Read only.

No editing.

No reactions.

No sharing.

A single button exists:

"Return to Silence"

Clicking it causes the manuscript to slowly dissolve into floating particles.

---

# Hall Behaviour

Every entrusted inheritance lights one deepa.

The hall slowly transforms over time.

The more inheritances exist:

* warmer atmosphere
* more illuminated carvings
* stronger feeling of life

If every deepa niche is occupied:

No additional deepas light.

The hall has reached its full collective inheritance.

---

# Motion Principles

Every movement is slow.

Every transition is deliberate.

The only sudden movement is the manuscript launching into the sky.

That contrast should surprise the user.

---

# Sound

Very subtle:

Wind

Deepa crackling

Stone ambience

Distant temple bells

Soft cloth movement

Silence is preferred over music.
